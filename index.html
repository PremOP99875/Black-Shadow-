BGMI Tournament Rules

Players mjust follow these rules:

  1. Players must use a registered account only.
  2. Each team consists of 4 players (plus 1 optional substitute).
  3. Emulators are NOT allowed—only mobile devices permitted.
  4. Any use of hacks, cheats, or third-party software results in an instant ban.

Match Format:

  • Winner (1st Place): 15 Points
  • 2nd Place: 12 Points
  • 3rd Place: 10 Points
  • 4th-10th Place: 5-8 Points
  • Per Kill: 1 Point

Free Fire Tournament Rules

General Rules

  • Players must use their registered Free Fire account.
  • Each team consists of 4 players (plus 1 optional substitute).
  • Emulators are NOT allowed—only mobile devices are permitted
  • Any use of hacks, cheats, or third-party software results in an instant ban
  • Players must follow the given lobby code and timing—failure to join in time leads to disqualification.
  • Toxic behavior, offensive language, or teaming with opponents is strictly prohibited

Valorant Tournament Rules

General Rules:

  1. Players must use their registered Riot ID.
  2. Each team consists of 5 players (plus 1 optional substitute).
  3. Only PC is allowed—any third-party software or cheats will result in disqualification.
  4. Toxicity, offensive language, and unsportsmanlike behavior will lead to penalties or bans.
  5. Pauses are limited to technical issues only and must be requested with valid reasons.

Match Format and Scoring:

  • Best-of-1 for early rounds, Best-of-3 for semifinals, and Best-of-5 for finals
  • Standard Competitive Format (13 rounds to win, switching sides after 12 rounds).
  • Overtime Rules: If 12-12, teams must win by 2 rounds.
